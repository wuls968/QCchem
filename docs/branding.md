chemistry, clear provenance, and trust boundaries before claims.

## Direction

The selected direction is "trusted quantum": a minimal quantum orbital or
molecular-bond motif paired with a verification/check or precision-grid cue.
It should read as a technical research software mark rather than a generic atom
illustration.

Recommended palette:

- Deep scientific blue: `#0f62fe`
- Deep navy: `#002d9c`
- Verification green: `#198038`
- Paper background: `#f2f4f8`
- Primary text: `#161616`
